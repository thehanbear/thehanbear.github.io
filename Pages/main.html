About me

I’ve always been curious about how people experience the digital world. And that curiosity led me into UI/UX design where I saw how a simple interaction could spark comfort, clarity, or even joy. Wanting to bring those ideas to life myself, I began learning to code.

Through Hive Helsinki, I built a good foundation in C and C++, gaining the discipline of classical programming. Now, I’m expanding into modern front-end and emerging technologies, I build digital experiences that connect design and code with clarity and warmth.

Living and working in Asia and Europe, in many different roles, taught me how to get back up and keep going, even when things feel clumsy or difficult. That resilience helps me empathize with users, tackle challenges, and seek better ways to build meaningful experiences.

I find beauty in simple moments.. The sound of rain on a tent, any kind of bear, a quiet fire, the warmth of my dogs’ eyes. Those things remind me why I love building products that bring a little comfort, joy, and connection into someone’s day.

Not every day feels easy,
but every day is a good day to begin.
To learn, to build, and to make
someone’s experience a little brighter.
